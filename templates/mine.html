Mine a New Block

Mining Content:

During the mining process, your server is working to find a valid proof-of-work for the new block. Here are the key steps:

  1. Verify pending transactions.
  2. Find a valid proof-of-work for the new block.
  3. Create a new block with the proof and add it to the blockchain.
  4. Receive a mining reward.

Click the "Mine Now" button to initiate the mining process.
